Privacy Policy

Last updated: 25 April 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Goyal Ji (operated by JP Industries, "we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects information you provide through this website. We are committed to handling your data lawfully under India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act).

1. Information we collect

When you fill in a contact or callback form on this site, we collect:

  • Name
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Company name (where provided)
  • Buyer role (distributor / wholesaler / retailer / institutional / other)
  • Products of interest, preferred callback time, and your message

We also collect basic, non-personal usage data via Google Analytics 4 (anonymised page views, device type, approximate region).

2. How we use it

  • To respond to your enquiry and discuss potential supply or distribution arrangements.
  • To follow up on quotes, samples, or orders you have requested.
  • To improve the website (analytics only, in aggregate).

We do not use your information for marketing emails, automated profiling, or any purpose unrelated to your enquiry.

3. Where it is stored

Form submissions are processed through Web3Forms, which forwards each submission to our business email jpindustries108@gmail.com. We do not maintain a separate customer database on this site. Analytics data is held by Google in line with their Privacy Policy.

4. Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for marketing. We may share it only with our service providers (e.g. Web3Forms, Google Analytics) strictly to operate this website, or where required by law.

5. Retention

We retain enquiry information for as long as needed to respond to you and maintain a record of business communication, and we delete it on request (see "Your rights" below).

6. Your rights

Under the DPDP Act and applicable law, you may:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Withdraw consent for further communication.

To exercise any of these rights, email jpindustries108@gmail.com with the subject line "Privacy Request" and we will respond within 30 days.

7. Cookies

We use only essential cookies and Google Analytics cookies for aggregate usage measurement. The site does not deploy advertising cookies or cross-site tracking pixels. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

8. Children

This site is intended for business buyers (distributors, wholesalers, retailers, institutional buyers). We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18.

9. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect any change.
